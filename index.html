SoulReads

SoulReads is an online book club built with React and Express. It allows for members to keep an online library and book journal, and also allows for member interaction through a public member forum. This was a complete Full-Stack application developed by a team of myself and two other amazing women! My primary role was constructing our entire front-end with ReactJS, developing everything from the components, to the visual conception/layout, and the full visual design. This project was completed in terms of a capstone presentation, but will continue to be fully developed and pushed to market.
